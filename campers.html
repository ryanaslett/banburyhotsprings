Welcome to Banbury Hot Springs

To insure that all our campers will have a pleasant and enjoyable stay with us, we ask that the campground rules listed below be observed.

NOT RESPONSIBLE - Management and Ownership are not responsible for anything lost or stolen. Also, not responsible in any way for any accident or injury. Gates on driveway are closed and locked from 10:30 p.m. till 7:00 a.m. If there is a possibility that you will need to exit the locked gate - re: for an emergency - you will need to contact management during pool and office hours. If you do not, and gates are damaged, you will be financially responsible for repairs.

OFFICE HOURS - The campground office is located in the pool area, please use the main pool gate only. The office is open from 12:00 noon to 9:00 p.m. Please settle accounts during these hours.

OFFICE HOURS and CHECKOUT TIME - Quiet hours are to be observed 10:30 p.m. to 7:00 a.m. Please dim your lights during this period. Our checkout time is 3:00 p.m. Please advise usif you plan to stay another day. Generator Use Prohibited.

GATES - The campground gates is to be kept closed. The gate on the main driveway is locked at 10:30 p.m. and opened at 7:00 a.m. for your security.

RESTROOMS and SHOWERS - The restrooms, showers and laundromat are located by the campground gate. Their use is restricted to registered campers.

POOL - Registered campers get a discount on daily pool fee. You must identify yourself as a registered camper. The pool is open from 12:00 noon to 8:45 p.m. with a lifeguard on duty. We get people out of the pool\hot baths at 8:45 p.m..

SUPERVISION of CHILDREN - Please help us keep our restrooms and locker rooms clean. Children under 6 should not be in these areas unless accompanied by an adult. No children under 6 are allowed in the pool area without an adult.

MOTOR VEHICLES - Observe the 10 m.p.h. speed limit and keep a careful watch for children. Motorbikes, motorcycles and ATV's are not to be ridden in the campground.

PETS - Do keep pets on a leash. Do not leave your pet unattended in the campground. Pets are not permitted in the pool area. You Must Clean Up After Your Pet.

FIRES - Confine fires to fireplaces and grills. Do not build fires on the campground.

REFUSE - Please place all trash in collection containers.

EMERGENCIES - In the event of an emergency, notify office when open or call 911 from cell.

FACILITIES - Our large outdoor swimming pool is constantly fed with clean, clear mineral water. It's temperature is adjusted daily for your comfort. Private hot tubs and jacuzzies are available. They are drained, cleaned and disinfected between each use. We have private picnic areas for rent and a boat ramp on the Snake River.
